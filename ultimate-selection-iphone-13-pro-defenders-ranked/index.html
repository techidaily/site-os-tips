What to Look for in an iPhone Case in 2024

A modern flagship phone like the iPhone 13 Pro is a significant investment, and you’ll want to protect it as best you can. Of course, the iPhone 13 is pretty tough out of the box, so some brave individuals don’t feel the need to have a case at all. For the rest of us, the dangers of daily life mean that you always run the risk of dropping your phone accidentally or in a moment of inattention.

While all phone cases provide some form of protection for your device, they don’t offer the same degree or type of protection. You need to carefully choose a case that strikes the right balance between look, feel, and functionality. Think about how and where you use your phone and match that with the most appropriate case.

The first choice you’ll have to make is what material you want for the case. The main choices are soft silicone, clear plastic, and leather. Some cases, such as those ruggedized against serious hazards, may use carbon fiber or other rigid materials.

Silicone cases offer the least added bulk and preserve the slimline feel of a phone. They’re great for preventing scratches and dents, but there’s limited protection against drop damage since they are so thin.

Hard plastic clear cases do the same, but they also provide better impact protection than soft silicone cases. They aren’t quite as lovely to hold as silicone but offer a good mix of looks and protection. If you pick a clear plastic case, you can also show off your Apple iPhone 13 Pro’s colorway.

Ruggedized cases are meant for people who spend time in rough places, such as construction sites. Modern smartphones, especially high-end ones, have some intrinsic water and dust resistance . Specialized cases aren’t necessary for most people, but they’re practically necessary for those that do need them.

Finally, leather cases offer some of the best overall protection. Leather does weather naturally over time, and not everyone likes that, but if you want something that can take a beating and feels good to hold, it’s a good premium option.

With all of this in mind, here are our iPhone 13 Pro case recommendations.

The cases below only fit the standard iPhone 13 Pro model. They will not fit the iPhone 13 Mini , the standard iPhone 13 , or iPhone 13 Pro Max .

Best iPhone 13 Pro Case Overall: Smartish Gripmunk

Smartish

Pros Cons
Excellent grip Not as protective as rugged cases
Microfiber on the inside
Multiple color and design variants
Most color variants are MagSafe-compatible

The Smartish Gripmunk is a no-frills but excellent case for the iPhone 13 Pro. As the name suggests, it’s designed to offer superior grip and includes textured sides for the same. Besides its grippy nature, the Smartish case is also great at keeping the iPhone safe from everyday mishaps.

In other highlights, the case features raised edges around the screen and the camera module to protect both from scratches and scrapes. There are also air pockets on the corners to aid in drop protection. Plus, while its relatively slim profile doesn’t add too much bulk to the phone, it makes it slightly less protective than more rugged and thick cases.

Another great thing about the case is its microfiber lining that ensures that the case itself doesn’t end up leaving blemishes on your phone’s exterior. You will also be happy to know you can get the Smartish Gripmunk in eight color and design options, six of which are MagSafe compatible. Additionally, you can use the customizable variant to add one of the available pattern designs to your case.

Best MagSafe iPhone 13 Pro Case: Apple Clear Case with MagSafe

Apple

Pros Cons
MagSafe certified Clear cases don’t offer the best grip
You can see your phone in all its glory
Every MagSafe accessory will match a clear case

Apple and third-party sellers are churning out a variety of MagSafe accessories, but not all of them are going to look terrific attached to a MagSafe Silicone Case . If you’re planning on really investing in the MagSafe ecosystem, it makes more sense to go for the Apple Clear Case with MagSafe .

The logic is simple—anything and everything looks great with a clear case. This is one of the few clear cases guaranteed to be compatible with MagSafe accessories both physically and aesthetically.

Thanks to a special coating, it’s also scratch-resistant, which deals with a common complaint regarding hard clear cases. You also get the same precise magnetic-aligned fit, and the material is treated to prevent yellowing.

The only real downside to a clear case like this is a lack of grip, so be careful when putting it down on your leg or a sloped surface. Then again, if it does fall, this clear polycarbonate material will do a good job of making sure the phone isn’t damaged.

Best Rugged iPhone 13 Pro Case: Otterbox Defender Series

Otterbox

You have a tough job, and that means you need a tough phone case to keep your device working. If you’ve set your heart on an iPhone 13 Pro but think it’s too risky to take it where you need to go, this rugged Defender Case from Otterbox is just what you need.

The Defender Series uses a multi-layer design to ensure that forces from drops and bumps are diffused through the case and not through your delicate phone and its components. According to the store page, the Defender stands up to four times the drop protection that qualifies as military-grade. It also has raised edges to ensure your camera and screen don’t touch the ground when the case is flat.

Speaking of the screen, this does not come with a screen protector, as many rugged cases do, but it is compatible with Otterbox’s screen protectors for the iPhone 13 Pro.

You might not use your Lightning port much thanks to wireless charging and AirDrop, but you don’t want it filling up with crud either. The Defender case comes with port protection. Just make sure that you need this level of protection because the Defender does add more thickness and bulk than standard phone cases.

If you need less protection and less bulk, there are other options .

If you are looking for a durable clear case for your iPhone 13 Pro, it’s hard to go wrong with the Speck GemShell . It’s built to withstand drops from as high as eight feet but still has a slim design that supports wireless charging. Its dual-layer bumper helps protect the iPhone from impacts and other mishaps. You also get raised edges, precise cutouts, and tactile buttons.

Plus, the transparent design is perfect for flaunting the color and design of your iPhone 13 Pro. You can also choose from 10 other variants of the case, which have a color or design thrown in over the transparent look. Additionally, there is a MagSafe-compatible version of the GemShell if you want to use MagSafe accessories.

Unfortunately, the Speck offering is slightly expensive. So, if you don’t want to shell out for the GemShell, you can consider the Tech21 Evo Clear . It offers similarly solid protection with a transparent design. But it costs significantly less.
