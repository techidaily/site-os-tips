1 Power off your Android phone.

2 Press and hold down the "Volume -" key and "Power" key simultaneously for a few seconds.

3 Your Android phone will boot, and then you can enter the Recovery Mode. You can use "Volume" keys to select options from the menu.

4 Select "wipe data/factory reset" and tap on it. This step will wipe all settings and files on your Android device. Then your device will reboot, and the Android password will be removed.
