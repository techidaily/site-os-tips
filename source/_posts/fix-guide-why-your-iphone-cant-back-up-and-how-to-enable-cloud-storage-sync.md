## 6 Tips for You to Fix iCloud 'Cannot Restore Backup' to iPhone 7/8/8 Plus/X

 In case of data overwritten or loss, so people would like to back up their iPhone data. By default, there are two inbuilt backup ways for iPhone. One is iTunes and the other one is iCloud. iCloud seems very important in iPhone data backups for many iOS users. However, many of you may encounter some issues when attempting to restore iPhone backups from iCloud, like "Cannot Restore Backup" prompt.
