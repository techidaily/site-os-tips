##  What to Look for in an iPhone 15 Leather Case

 Unlike regular [iPhone 15 cases](https://review-topics.techidaily.com/itel-p55plus-unlock-tool-remove-android-phone-password-pin-pattern-and-fingerprint-by-drfone-android-unlock-android-unlock/), leather cases are classy and luxurious. However, certain factors are worth considering when shopping for the best iPhone 15 leather cases.

 Fit is the first thing to consider when looking for an iPhone 15 leather case to buy. We've selected the best leather cases for iPhone 15, and yes, they fit perfectly. A good iPhone 15 leather case should also protect your phone against sweat, scratches, scuffs, smudges, spills, and other day-to-day wear and tear at the bare minimum.

 Finishing is another key factor to consider when buying iPhone 15 leather cases. You'll want to get leather cases with premium stitching, styling, and finish that also patina (age well). Aesthetics is a big reason to pick up a leather case, so the last thing you want is a case that will degrade in terms of looks over time.

 The [iPhone 15](https://fox-info.techidaily.com/15-sites-offering-premium-free-vectr-and-illustration-tools-and-services/) is MagSafe compatible and also supports wireless charging—most cases support these features, but not all of them. If this is important to you, make sure to double-check that your chosen case is MagSafe compatible! There's not much of a point to a case if you need to keep taking it off.

 While shopping for leather iPhone 15 cases, you might expect to spend a little more than usual. Surprisingly, the best leather cases for iPhone 15 in this roundup are all reasonably priced.

## FAQ

### How do I clean and maintain my leather iPhone 15 case?

 Apple recommends using a clean cloth with warm water and mild hand soap to clean leather iPhone case. A mild cleaner along with a dry clean cloth will also do. Note that leather cleaners and conditioners can discolor your leather iPhone case, so avoid them.

### Will MagSafe work with leather iPhone cases?

 Yes, MagSafe works with leather iPhone cases. In fact, all the iPhone 15 leather cases in this roundup are MagSafe-friendly except the FLAUNT CASES iPhone 15 Case. Note however, that the iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone 15 Pro Max FLAUNT CASES Faux Leather Cases have MagSafe options.

### Will an iPhone 14 case fit on an iPhone 15?

 Yes, an iPhone 14 case can fit an iPhone 15 but not perfectly. Although both phones share the same 6.1-inch display and other similarities in form factor, the iPhone 15 is 1.1mm taller with contour edges. That being said, if you want a 100 percent perfect fit your iPhone, then get an iPhone 15 case.

### Why did Apple discontinue its leather cases?

 According to [Lisa Jackson](https://www.independent.co.uk/tech/apple-leather-strap-watch-iphone-case-b2410462.html), Apples's V.P of Environment, Policy, and Social Initiatives, Apple is ditching leather for more carbon-neutral materials. This decision affects iPhone cases and watch bands going forward.
