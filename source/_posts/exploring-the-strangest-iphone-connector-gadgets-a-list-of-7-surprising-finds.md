_Digital Eye Center_

 Lastly, perhaps the most specific iPhone adapter out there. A "slit lamp" is an instrument used by ophthalmologists to shine a thin sheet of light into the eye. It makes it possible to examine the front and back of the eye.

 The [slit lamp adapter](https://www.digitaleyecenter.com/product/iphone-slit-lamp-adapter/?gclid=CjwKCAiAk--dBhABEiwAchIwkalQxHnUt-%5FJ5h0iknEv7p-0WmNUg4-NjEG0qQm8lceOVj9cdBejPxoCTbQQAvD%5FBwE) allows slit lamps to be aligned with the iPhone's camera and to take digital photos of the eye. It includes three sleeves for different lamp diameters and a remote shutter button. Do you need this? Probably not. but it's very cool for a specific use.
