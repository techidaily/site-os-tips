## Export Video, Photo, Contact with Password on Mac

1\. To encrypt photo during transferring, go to **Photo** interface > choose photos you for exporting to Mac > tick the box **Export with Encryption** \> and then press **Export**.

2\. Exporting video with encryption use a similar process as encrypting photos. Click into **Video** manager > select videos you for exporting > click **Export with Encryption** right beside **Select All** \> Export.

3\. To encrypt contacts, go to the **Contact** center > select contacts you want to export and encrypt > check **Export with Encryption** \> and click Export.
