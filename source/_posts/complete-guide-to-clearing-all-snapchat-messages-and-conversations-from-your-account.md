## Part 2: How to delete Snapchat history

 Snapchat conversations can be deleted on iPhone, iPad, or Android, through the app settings by following the default steps listed below.

![Delete Snapchat History on iPhone Android](https://www.aiseesoft.com/images/resource/delete-snapchat-history/how-to-delete-snapchat-history-on-iphone-android.jpg)

Step 1: Launch Snapchat app and navigate to "Settings".

Step 2: Select "Clear Conversation" under the "Account Action" section.

Step 3: You can delete your conversations one after the other.

Step 4: If you prefer to delete all Snapchat conversation with one click, you can tap "Clear All" under "Account Action".

 It is necessary to mention that deleting Snapchat conversation with this method will not clear all Snapchat histories. You can still find other traces of Snapchat, such as saved files, videos, pictures, and some cached contents.
