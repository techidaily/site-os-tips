##  Open the iPhone's Scientific Calculator

 First, open the "Calculator" app. If you can't find it, swipe downward with one finger in the middle of the screen to see a search bar. Type "calc" and select the "Calculator" app icon when it appears.

![Searching for 'Calculator'](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/1-search-calculator-1.png) 

 Once Calculator opens, you'll probably see a screen like this. It's a simple interface with a grid of numbers and basic mathematical operators. Hardly scientific at all!

![The iPhone Calculator app in a vertical orientation, showing normal mode.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/calc_vertical.jpg)

 If your calculator doesn't change into scientific mode when you rotate your iPhone, then it's likely that you have orientation lock enabled. To disable it, [pull up Control Center](https://youtube-blog.techidaily.com/ring-how-tseries-harnesses-revenue-from-online-viewer-base-youtube/) and tap the orientation lock icon until it is no longer highlit.
