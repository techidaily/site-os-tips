##  How to See Hidden Photos on iPhone

 To find your hidden photos, first, launch the Photos app on your iPhone.

 At the bottom of the Photos app, tap "Albums."

![Tap the &quot;Albums&quot; button.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/tap-the-albums-button.png) 

 Scroll down to the bottom of the albums list and tap the "Hidden" album in the Utilities section.

 (On older versions of iOS, the "Hidden" album is located under Other Albums rather than Utilities.)

 If you do not see the "Hidden" album option, the album itself might be hidden. To enable it, follow the steps in the [section below](https://android-pokemon-go.techidaily.com/in-2024-how-to-come-up-with-the-best-pokemon-team-on-htc-u23-pro-drfone-by-drfone-virtual-android/).

![Tap &quot;Hidden&quot; under Utilities](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/hidden-album.png) 

 The "Hidden" album screen shows all your hidden photos and videos.

![View iPhone's hidden photos.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/3-hidden-photos-iphone.png)

##  Enable the Hidden Photo Album on iPhone

 Starting in iOS 14, you can [turn off the "Hidden" album](https://instagram-video-recordings.techidaily.com/2024-approved-unlocking-the-potential-storing-insta-media-on-your-phone/) in the Photos app. To re-enable that album, you will have to change an option in your iPhone's settings.

 To do so, open [the Settings app](https://some-knowledge.techidaily.com/updated-harmonizing-music-posting-with-legal-standards/) on your iPhone and tap "Photos." Then, enable the "Show Hidden Album" option. Your album is now visible in the Photos app, and you can access your hidden photos.

![Tap &quot;Hidden&quot; under Utilities](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/hidden-album.png) 

 And that's how you go about finding photos and videos that you previously hid on your iPhone. Enjoy!
