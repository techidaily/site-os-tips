 The easiest way to print text messages from iPhone and Android is to take screenshots of the conversation and print them. There are alternative methods that use third-party apps for both platforms, too.

##  How to Print Text Messages From iPhone

 First, [take a screenshot on your iPhone](https://location-social.techidaily.com/simple-and-effective-ways-to-change-your-country-on-youtube-app-of-your-samsung-galaxy-a14-4g-drfone-by-drfone-virtual-android/) of the text conversation from the Messages app. Do this by pressing and holding the Power + Volume Up keys, or Power + Home button.
