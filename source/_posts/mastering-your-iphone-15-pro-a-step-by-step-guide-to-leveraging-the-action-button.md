## Mastering Your iPhone 15 Pro: A Step-by-Step Guide to Leveraging the Action Button

The release of [iPhone 15 Pro models](https://facebook-video-content.techidaily.com/new-direct-interaction-enhancing-live-broadcasts-with-desktop-share/) created quite a buzz, mainly because of the most awaited [switch to a USB-C port](https://tech-recovery.techidaily.com/the-complete-walkthrough-correctly-repairing-d3dx939dll-errors/). But one particular change that has captured an equal share of attention is the introduction of the Action button.

 Apple first introduced the Action button on the [Apple Watch Ultra](https://video-capture.techidaily.com/new-bandicam-in-depth-review-for-the-modern-user/). Now, it's launched a modified version for the iPhone 15 Pro series. It's a small button above the volume rockers, replacing the traditional Ring/Silent switch that's been on its predecessors.

##  What Is the Default iPhone Action Button Function?

 Just like the old Ring/Silent switch, the Action button switches your iPhone between Ring and Silent modes by default. All you have to do is press and hold the Action button to toggle between these modes.

 By doing so, you'll feel haptic feedback and see a Ring/Silent animation in the [Dynamic Island](https://youtube-zero.techidaily.com/024-approved-mastering-mobile-thumbnail-creation-for-youtube/) of your iPhone. This will let you know that the Action button has activated the Ring or Silent mode.

##  How Do You Change What the iPhone Action Button Does?

 The good news? Rather than just using it as a Ring/Silent switch, you can actually customize the Action button according to your needs and preferences.

 Here's how you can change the default function of the Action button on your iPhone:

 First, go to the Settings app on your iPhone and tap on the "Action Button" option.

![action button settings option in the iphone 15 pro's settings app](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/action-button-settings-1.png)

 Here’s the list of actions you can perform using the Action button:

* **Silent Mode:** Turns the Silent mode on or off like the Ring/Silent switch on the previous iPhone models.
* **Focus:** Activates or deactivates a particular [Focus mode](https://youtube-clips.techidaily.com/2024-approved-designing-distinctive-denouements/) (default mode is Do Not Disturb).
* **Camera:** Opens the Camera app to take a photo (default), selfie, video, portrait, or portrait selfie.
* **Flashlight:** Turns on/off the flashlight of your iPhone.
* **Voice Memo:** Starts or stops [recording a voice memo](https://hardware-tips.techidaily.com/mastering-modern-electronics-a-guide-by-toms-hardware-experts/) using the default Voice Memos app.
* **Magnifier:** Launches the default Magnifier app that turns the iPhone camera into a magnifying glass to zoom in on objects.
* **Shortcut:** Opens an app of your choice or runs a specific shortcut (we'll get to the details in just a minute!).
* **Accessibility:** Accesses various accessibility features like [Live Captions](https://driver-error.techidaily.com/hd-audio-mastery-on-windows-11/), [Guided Access](https://some-techniques.techidaily.com/2024-approved-from-camera-to-channel-a-thoroughly-researched-video-instruction-manual/), Zoom, and more.
* **No Action:** As the name suggests, it doesn't perform any function.

##  How to Customize the iPhone Action Button With Shortcuts

 As discussed earlier, you can create your own Action button shortcuts or open a particular app using Apple's very own Shortcut app. You can use it to activate your hotspot, turn on low power mode, run [Shazam](https://android-pokemon-go.techidaily.com/the-magnificent-art-of-pokemon-go-streaming-on-asus-rog-phone-8-pro-drfone-by-drfone-virtual-android/), or perform various other functions – the options with the Shortcuts are endless!

 For this example, we’ll show you how you can create a Shortcut to open a certain app.

 Swipe to the "Shortcuts" option and tap the "Choose a Shortcut" button.
